Desi Beats (80 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of Teams per Pool: 1
Duration of each performance: 6 - 8 minutes on stage including stage set up
Style: Bollywood
Appreciation Trophy: Best Dancer
Other rules and regulations:
  • Music piece including stage clearance should be 6-8 minutes.
  • No limit in no. of participants in the group dance
  • Each team will incorporate one restriction, which will be conveyed to the Galaxy Captains by 30 th January 2016. Examples of possible restrictions areo Performing a part of their dance while staying connected for no less than 30 seconds with at least 6 participants on stage.
    • At least 6 group members will have to be on their knees for no less than 30 seconds.
    • Perform a portion of their routine blindfolded for no less than 30 seconds with at least 6 participants on stage.
    • Perform a mirror image in their dance for no less than 30 seconds with at least 6 participants on stage.
  • These restrictions for the pools will be decided by lottery. Props may be used but carry no extra points.

Judging Criteria:

Choreography 30
Synchronization and Energy 20
Expressions/Attitude 15
Overall Impact 15
Restriction 10
Costume 10

Note: Overall impact (it includes how well the teams have brought about a filmy look to the dance)
Slam JAM (60 points)

No. of Pools: 4; Number of Teams per Pool: 1 (6-8 participants in each team)

Other rules and regulations:
  • Each pool battles once with all the 3 other pools one by one. Hence, there will be a total 6 matches of 6 minutes each.
  • In each battle, the teams will perform alternatively for 1 minute each and hence total of 3 minutes and the last round will be a solo/duo throw down.
  • Scores will be awarded to every team in each battle and team with higher points will be awarded the win.
  • Two points will be awarded for a win and 1 point for a draw (in case of equal score) and 0 for a loss.
  • After all 6 matches, the total points (out of 6) decide the position of the team.
  • In case of equal points team with higher total score (out of 300, 100 per battle) will be awarded a higher position.
  • Each participant is expected to perform for at least 30 seconds in a match.
  • A basket of 10- 20 songs will be revealed to the Pools from which any songs may be played in a random order.

Judging Criteria:

Choreography and Execution 10
Music Sense 20
Battle Spirit and Energy 20
Overall Impact 25
Power Moves, Stunts, Freezes 25
Takes Two To Tango (40 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of Teams per Pool: 2 (Y14 and Y15 only)
Duration: Maximum of 4 minutes of Audio file
Other rules and regulations:
  • No repetition of participants is allowed.
  • Unconventional Dance forms will be encouraged.
  • Restrictions
    • 1st Team: No restriction
    • 2nd Team: Each team will incorporate a restriction or a prop, decided by lottery by 20th January.
      Examples of possible restrictions are:-
      • Furniture
      • Props like Yoga Ball, Trolley Bags etc.
      • Right Leg of a member to be tied with the Left leg of another through a rope of 2m
  • Points Distribution
    • 1st Team - 40%
    • 2nd Team - 60%

Judging Criteria:

Choreography / Restriction / Innovation 30
Onstage Chemistry 20
Energy 20
Stage coverage 10
Overall Impact 15
Costume 5

Other rules and regulations:

  • These files shall not be disclosed before.

Choreography 30

Overall Impact 25
