ELS events

Creative Writing (30 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. Of Teams from each Pool: 2 (max 2 participants in each team)
Appreciation Trophy: Best Writer, Short English Fiction
  • Each team will submit two entries which may consist of multiple compositions.
  • Total time allowed is 2 hours.
  • There will also be a prescribed word count.
  • Total points for each pool would be the sum of points of both teams from the pool.
  • Decision of the judge will be final.
Dumb Charades (35 points)

No. of Pools: 4
  • Open participation for Prelims
    • 3 members in a team
    • Minimum 3 and Maximum 5 teams from a Pool
    • Finals: 5 teams from each pool (No. of teams in each round can vary)
    • Total points (for each pool)
      • 25% points of top 5 teams from the pool in prelims
      • 75% points of 5 teams that participated in finals
    • There will be several rounds including Blitz, Bidding, Handicap, Triple DumbC and a few surprise rounds.
Word Games (35 points)

No. of Pools: 4
  • Written Round
    • Open participation
    • Duration of 2 hours
    • A minimum of 3 teams from each pool consisting of max 3 members each
JAM (30 points)

No. of Pools: 4
  • Prelims
    • 4 participants from each pool out of which at least 2 should be Y15 and 1 should be from Y14 and older batches.
  • Finals
    • 2 participants selected from the prelims
  • Total points (for each pool)
    • 25% points from prelims of both finalists
    • 75% points of the highest ranked participant from the pool in the finals Decision of the JAMmaster will be final
  • The Coordinators of the Club will be Spotter
Mixed Bag (20 points)

No. of Pools: 4
  • It will be an A/V round with 2 teams (max 3 members each) from each pool
  • May consist of a variety of events. For ex.
    • Literature quiz
    • Spell bee
    • Pop-cult quiz
    • Poetry recitation
    • Group extempore
    • Literary treasure hunt
      Etc.
