Miscellaneous Events

Galaxy Parade (70 points)

No. of Pools: 4
Rules:
  • Route: Road connecting Hall 2-1-3-4-swimming pool to New SAC
  • The Galaxy Captain and hall captains shall guide the route
  • Theme: Every pool will submit 4 themes of its choice on any issue by 23th January 2016 arranged by preference. After that, the themes will be checked by the Galaxy managers. In case there is any problem, the pools will be notified and asked to change their theme. The lottery will take place out of these themes on 25th January 2016.
  • Use of obscene words or signs shall not be entertained and reported to the judges
  • Use of external human source is not allowed
Pool Flag (15 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of entries per Pool: 1
Rules:
  • The flag sample will be given to the judges.
  • Also have to submit the jpg. file of Flag design.
  • There is no restriction on flag theme or shape. However, the flag dimensions must not exceed 1350 x 900 mm (Standard Size Number 5 of the Indian National Flag)
Antakshari (30 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of teams per Pool: 2 (3 members each)
Rules:

Bollywood songs only



Pool Video (15 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of videos per pool: 1
Video Length: 6 minutes maximum
Rules:

Each team has to shoot a video about its pool over the time of preparation and competition and shall present the video on the final day.



Dubsmash (10 points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of entries per pool: 5 (Y12 and Older batches only)
Rules:
  • No repetition of participants is allowed
  • Make the most dramatic, funny and innovative dubsmashes
  • All 5 entries will be judged and the total will be used to rank the pools
Mr/Ms Galaxy (30 Points)

No. of Pools: 4; No. of videos per pool: 1
Appreciation Trophy: Mr/Ms Galaxy
Rules:
  • The participant will be judged on his skills in the following fields:
    • Dance : Solo ; 3 minutes
    • Dramatics : Mono Acting; 5 min
    • Music: Solo Singing or Play an Instrument
    • Fine Arts: Sketching or Painting
    • Quiz: Mixed Quiz
    • Literary skills: Poetry Slam
  • All the fields have equal weightage
